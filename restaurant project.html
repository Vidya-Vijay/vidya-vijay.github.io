Reviving Restaurant Sales: A Data-Driven Approach Using Predictive Analytics and Business Insights

A predictive analytics project to support a local Thai restaurant's business turnaround

Course: BUS 424 – Customer Intelligence

Project Overview

Partnered with a family-owned Thai restaurant in Abbotsford facing declining sales and customer satisfaction. Leveraged 313 days of structured sales, weather, and calendar data plus 400+ Google reviews to diagnose issues, predict sales, and design targeted interventions, aiming to reverse revenue decline and improve customer loyalty.

Key Features

  • Built an end-to-end analytics solution integrating structured (POS sales, weather) and unstructured (Google reviews) datasets.
  • Cleaned and merged multi-source data using SQL and Python.
  • Designed interactive Power BI dashboards with dynamic filters, KPI cards, and storytelling visuals to guide owner decisions.
  • Developed a Multiple Linear Regression model (RMSE: 276.99) to predict daily revenue based on temperature, day-of-week, and holidays.
  • Used NLP & sentiment analysis (NLTK) to correlate customer tone with tipping behavior, identifying a 20% drop in tips following staff changes.
  • Created word clouds revealing “delicious” & “friendly” as loyalty drivers, while “messed” & “disappointed” flagged service gaps.

    Key Insights Driving Recommendations

    • Slow Thursdays: Lowest sales; recommended weekday lunch specials & event tie-ins.
    • Weather sensitivity: Warm days + holidays spiked revenue; proposed outdoor seating promotion during warm seasons.
    • Negative customer sentiment grew after staff changes and linked to lower tips
    • Staff sentiment link: Negative reviews post-staff change directly tied to tipping decline; advised service retraining + private QR feedback channel.

    Business Impact Potential

    Projected 10–15% revenue uplift through targeted promotions, sentiment-driven feedback loops, and operational optimization, all achievable with minimal overhead for the owner.

    Conclusion

    Overall, this project strengthened my skills in data storytelling, model validation, and translating business problems into analytical solutions, while also reinforcing the power of analytics in small business decision-making.
