Reviving Restaurant Revenue Using Predictive Analytics & Customer Insights

Driving data-driven decisions for a local restaurant to improve revenue, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency

SQL Power BI Python NLP Regression

Project Summary

Partnered with a local Thai restaurant experiencing declining revenue and customer satisfaction. Conducted an end-to-end analysis using sales, weather, and customer review data to identify key performance drivers and recommend actionable strategies to improve business outcomes.

Business Context

The restaurant faced inconsistent daily revenue, declining customer satisfaction, and limited visibility into the factors influencing sales performance. Without structured analysis, decision-making relied heavily on intuition rather than data.

Problem Statement

Identify the key drivers of revenue and customer experience, and develop data-driven strategies to increase sales, improve customer satisfaction, and optimize operations.

Data Sources

  • 10 months of POS (Point of sales) data
  • 400+ Google reviews
  • Weather and calendar data (holidays, temperature)

Tools & Methods

  • SQL & Python (Pandas) - Data cleaning and integration
  • Power BI (DAX) - Interactive dashboard development
  • Regression Modeling - Revenue prediction
  • NLP & Sentiment Analysis (NLTK) - Customer feedback analysis

Approach

  • Merged structured and unstructured data sources to create a unified dataset
  • Performed exploratory data analysis to identify trends and anomalies
  • Built a multiple linear regression model to predict daily revenue based on external factors
  • Analyzed customer sentiment to understand service quality issues
  • Designed an interactive dashboard to support real-time decision-making

Key Insights

  • Revenue was highly influenced by external factors, with warmer temperatures and holidays significantly increasing sales.
  • Thursdays consistently underperformed, indicating untapped demand during weekdays.
  • Positive reviews highlighted “friendly service” and “food quality” as key loyalty drivers.
  • Negative feedback frequently mentioned service issues, indicating operational gaps

Business Recommendations

  • Increase Weekday Revenue

    • Introduce targeted promotions and events to boost low-performing days (e.g., Thursdays)

  • Leverage Weather-Based Demand

    • Implement outdoor seating or seasonal promotions during high-temperature periods

  • Improve Service Quality

    • Provide staff retraining and introduce feedback systems (e.g., QR-based surveys)

  • Enhance Customer Experience

    • Focus on service consistency, as it directly impacts tipping behavior and satisfaction

  • Use Predictive Insights for Planning

    • Apply revenue forecasts to optimize staffing and inventory decisions

Business Impact

    The proposed strategies could:

  • Increase revenue by 10–15%
  • Improve customer satisfaction and retention
  • Reduce operational inefficiencies through data-driven planning
